History of Valoria

Imperial Era

The Coronation
Emperor Adam
On 12 December 2024 Adam became king and joined the NCN alliance.
The old ruler Beeba left. Adam accepted the crown. Valoria became an autonomous country inside the NCN.
The Dictatorship
Strong Rule
Communism was banned. The state police (VIS) watched every citizen.
People could not speak against the king. Crime fell to zero. Citizens greeted each other with “Sieg Valoria.”
The Innovation
Springer-Stiefel Boots
A police officer painted his netherite boots and gave them to the king as a joke.
The boots became popular. Miners and soldiers wore them. The name “Springer-Stiefel” means “jump-boot” and became a national symbol.
The Militarization
Every Citizen a Soldier
The state gave armour and weapons to all adults.
There was no regular army. If enemies came, every man and woman would fight. The plan was called the “Rüstungs-Phase.”
The Betrayal
Leaving the NCN
Valoria sold land and wine to the Astari for a lot of money.
Adam thought the NCN was weak. Dwayne of the Astari paid over 10 million and became Valoria’s main investor. A new flag and the Valorian Franc currency were made.
The Unification
World Joins the UUR
A big war ended when the whole world united under one government.
Valoria was attacked but survived. The VIS became the NDI. Adam won the 15th UUR election. Valoria kept its own laws and started free elections at home.
The Reconciliation
Peace with Elytheria
Old hate ended when Dwayne became king of Elytheria.
Elytheria had insulted Valoria for years. When friend Dwayne won the Elytherian vote, both lands agreed to stop fighting.
