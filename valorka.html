Our Story

Valoria is among the largest wine producers of the world. When the nation chose a new path—abandoning the NCN alliance—it sold the first casks of "Valorka" to the Astari for millions of gold. The Astari investor Dwayne became Valoria's biggest partner, injecting over 10 million into the young economy. With that gold the Valorian National Bank was raised, the Franc was minted, and the new Valorian flag was raised for the first time. Every bottle of Valorka carries that moment of independence.

Only Valorian resources are pressed for the royal wine. Each cask is produced in the heart of the Valorian wine production: Frostborne.
